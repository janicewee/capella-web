A: No! You own your website forever. I use free hosting (GitHub Pages) so there are no ongoing costs unless you want maintenance services.

A: Photos of your work/service, your business information, and any existing customer reviews. I handle all the technical stuff.

A: Yes! Every website includes WhatsApp booking, tap-to-call, and contact forms so customers can reach you easily.
