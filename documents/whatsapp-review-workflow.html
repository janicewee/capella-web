Tips for Success | 成功秘诀

🎯 Do This:
  • Ask happy customers immediately after service (while they're looking in the mirror)
    服务后立即询问满意顾客（当他们照镜子时）
  • Make it easy: "Can I send you a quick link to leave a review?"
    让它简单："我可以发您一个快速链接留评论吗？"
  • Personalize messages: Use their name and mention their specific service
    个性化消息：使用他们的名字，提及具体服务
  • Aim for 1-2 reviews per week — that's 50+ new reviews per year!
    目标每周1-2条评论——每年50+新评论！
❌ Don't Do This | 不要这样做:
  • Ask unhappy customers for reviews
    请不满意的顾客留评
  • Send bulk requests to all customers at once
    一次性群发所有顾客
  • Ignore negative reviews
    忽略负面评论
  • Buy fake reviews
    购买假评论

Summary | 总结

Your Daily Workflow | 您的日常工作流程:
  1. After each appointment, check if customer was happy
    每次服务后，检查顾客是否满意
  2. If yes → Send review request message (2-3 hours later)
    如果是 → 发送评论请求消息（2-3小时后）
  3. Check Google Business daily for new reviews
    每天检查谷歌商业是否有新评论
  4. Reply to all reviews within 24 hours
    24小时内回复所有评论
  5. Keep QR code visible in salon at all times
    始终将二维码展示在店内

Time needed: 5-10 minutes per day | 所需时间：每天5-10分钟
